What is procrastination

The act of delaying something that you should do, usually because you do not want to do it
Academic procrastination is "student delay in studying or completing academic assignments" (Rotenstain et al., 2009).

Benefits:

  • immediate gratification
  • complete other tasks that have been put off
  • work faster to complete task at last minute
  • enhance concentration

Methods:

  1. Put away the task needed to be done
  2. Find something else to do
  3. When task cannot be further delayed, then do it
